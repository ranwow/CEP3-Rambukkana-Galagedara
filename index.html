Overlay Layers

Add KML, GeoJSON, or a zipped Shapefile (.zip containing .shp/.dbf/.shx/.prj) — e.g. tower line alignment or land boundaries. Toggle each on/off from the layer switcher on the map. DWG/DXF isn't supported by browsers; convert to one of the above first (e.g. via QGIS or AutoCAD export). Lines with a CAD-style Color attribute (AutoCAD Color Index) are drawn using that color automatically, at a fixed 0.5mm weight.

Data source

Connected to the live NBRI Google Sheet (tab: CEP 3 Rambukkana-Galagedara). The map re-fetches automatically on load and every 5 minutes while open. To point it at a different tab or sheet, edit SHEET_CSV_URL near the top of the script.
